Meet Sparkle

My yoga practice is radical, body loving movement medicine.

I am a yoga teacher, musician, healer, and artist from Oakland, CA.

I believe that yoga has become an infinite healing practice shaped through countless layers of diverse cultures, religions, and people over time. I bring this re-imagined, radically-inclusive view to others through musical, deeply creative yoga classes. Creativity is the most valuable gift I can offer, and when creativity comes through it reminds me of many blessings, many reasons to live, and many more to come. I vow to lead by example and live this message through fierce dedication and practice every single day
